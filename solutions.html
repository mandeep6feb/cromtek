Omni Channel Solutions

We have been working with different customers to provide solution which take care of their Omni channel strategies and enable their trade partners & consumer?s partners to be more productive and have consistent experience across the channels. Word is changing fast and different role and partners interact in organization in different ways across different media and devices hence they should treated in consistent manner in either way they interact. Some of the features of the solution provided to customers is as below:

  • Tracking of goods from company stocking to points to distributors and retailers.
  • Availability of information and suitable workflow over web browser or mobiles as per need of the roles.
  • Development of stocking policies and guidance to trade partner and field force on replenishment needs and providing needful alerts etc.
  • Mechanism of delivering product knowledge and training to last mile and ensuring that all the people in trade get latest and same information for effective sales & service process.
  • Needful solutions for supporting trade & promotions, stock checking, transparency in trade invoicing, control of authorized goods movements etc.
  • Facility of Route and beat Planning, Execution tracking, effectiveness, compliances, location tracking of field forces, verification of visits through GPS, auto route pick/drop suggestion based using GPS.
  • Capability of call center to manage and route calls, handle enquires & complaints and effective knowledge management for efficient call handling etc. Effective integration with social media and controlled communication over such media.

HRMS and Payroll

Over period of time we studied needs of companies and realized that organization need a web based platform which can cater to various needs, beyond core Order to Cash and Procure to Pay Cycles, of organization in cost effective and simplified manner. A high level overview of our solution is as given below:

  • Attendance Management taking care different modes of attendance punching like Biometric, Mobile, Manual, SMS etc. It also takes care of Out Door Duty, Leaves, Tours, and Regularization etc.
  • Tour and travel management with suitable workflows and intimation and data extract for taking the entries to ERP Systems like SAP, Oracle, Infor, Ramco, Tallly etc.
  • IT Asset Management, IT Service Management, Conference Room Booking, Meeting Schedules, Opinion Polls, Surveys, Knowledge Management, Document and Video Library, Organization Messaging etc.

  • Miscellaneous things, which company generally needs and those generally not provided by ERP/CRM systems, like Project Task Tracking, Progress Reporting, Holiday List, Employee Directory etc are provided with great simplicity in our solutions.
  • Lot of capability has been built for employee and management self-service with suitable workflow for approvals and intimations etc.

Hospital Management System

AROGYA is a Web Based Hospital Management system which has been developed targeting various requirements of a multi-specalty Hospitals who are not able to afford high end enterprise class ERP systems but want to streamline their operation using IT as key enabler. The key modules in the solution are as given below:

  • Patient
  • Ward Management
  • Billing
  • Inventory
  • PayRoll
  • Pathology & Laboratory
  • Query & Reporting

All the modules are very well integrated to share common data and at the same time segregates the operational activities of one module with another to provide proper flexibility & controls in the operations. Some of key features are:

  • Inventory Stock Level Notification Alarm
  • Intranet Based Emailing System
  • User defined Dynamic Security Policies

Technically speaking, the System has been developed to support Platform Independence thus is not binding the client to a specific hardware infrastructure. Additional terminals may be attached as easily as it is to add the system to the network because being web based no extra computer specific overhead is required. The System operates on a 3 tier architecture hence all the clients are thin clients with each and every database operation being handled by the backend web server that in turns communicates with the Database Server to perform the operation.
